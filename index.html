what does love mean to you?

“i miss u, goodbye” is more than a book or a magazine—it’s a visual and emotional experience that celebrates the beauty of the unfinished and the imperfect. It’s a collection of songs and poems that were never completed, captured directly from my notes app in their rawest and most honest form.

This project merges my two passions: art and design. On the one hand, it captures these unfinished songs’ intimate and spontaneous nature, embodying brief bursts of creativity. Conversely, design takes the forefront through a thoughtfully developed visual identity, branding, illustrations, and web design.

“i miss u, goodbye” transcends being merely a book or magazine; it offers a visual and emotional journey that honors the charm of the unfinished and the imperfect.

who am i?

My name is Nico de Vega, and I am an artist, writer, and designer based in Mexico. My work integrates visual creativity with written expression, delving into emotions and ideas across various disciplines. I have a strong passion for songwriting, writing, and design, viewing each project as a chance to narrate stories in a genuine and powerful manner.

As a multidisciplinary designer, I focus on branding, visual identity, web design, and illustration. I enjoy crafting visual experiences that embody identity and significance, merging artistic sensitivity with strategic planning to bring projects to fruition in both conceptual and aesthetic dimensions.
